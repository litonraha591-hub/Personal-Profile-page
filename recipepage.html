Tasty Recipe

Authentic Bangladeshi Pulao Recipe:

This exquisite dish brings together the rich aroma of Hilsa (Ilish) fish and fragrant chinigura/basmati rice. It is an absolute showstopper for festivals, weekends, or special family gatherings.

Ingredients You Will Need

Gather these ingredients divided into two main components: the fish marinade and the fragrant rice.

For the Ilish Fish Marinade:

  • Ilish Fish: 5-6 large pieces
  • Sour Yogurt(Tok Doi): 1/2 cup
  • Onion Paste: 3tbsp
  • Ginger Paste:1tsp
  • Garlic Paste:1/2 tsp
  • Salt: To Taste

For the Pulao Rice:

  • Pulao Rice:2 cups
  • Onion Slices:1/2 cup
  • Whole spices: 3 green cardamoms, 2 cinnamon sticks, 2 bay leaves

Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions
